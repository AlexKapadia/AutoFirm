## Relevant content
ISO/IEC 42001 specifies an **AI Management System (AIMS)** (Plan-Do-Check-Act). Logging/records-relevant requirements:
- **Event logging (Annex A control A.6.2.8 — "AI system recording of event logs"):** the organisation shall determine at which phases event logs are recorded and ensure AI systems record events; security/access-control events (intrusion detection, permission changes, authentication attempts) and data-handling movements (input, output, export, redaction, deletion) are to be captured and archived.
- **Documented information (clause 7.5):** controlled documents — AI policy, AIMS scope, risk assessment + treatment + Statement of Applicability, AI system impact assessments, competence records, operational monitoring + event logs, internal audit reports, management review outputs.
- **Records management:** policies must pre-define **retention and deletion**; process audit trails must be traceable; organisations maintain model cards, audit logs, and decision records to support **accountability and traceability** over the system lifecycle.
