## Key claims (the role taxonomy)
1. **Data Owner** — *accountable* for a data asset's "quality, security, and compliance"; sets
   policy for that asset; the decision-making authority.
2. **Data Steward** — works to "ensure that the data content and metadata are consistent with the
   organization's policies, standards, and business rules, resulting in an appropriate level of data
   quality." Appointed by / represents the owner; the *business-side* keeper of meaning, structure,
   and policy-conformance. (This is the closest match to AutoFirm's "organizer/librarian.")
3. **Data Custodian** — typically IT; *operational* keeper — "maintaining, archiving, recovering,
   backing up data, preventing data loss/corruption." Distinct from owner/steward (business roles).
4. **Separation principle:** accountability (owner) is separated from stewardship (steward, business
   meaning/policy) and from custody (custodian, operational/technical handling).
