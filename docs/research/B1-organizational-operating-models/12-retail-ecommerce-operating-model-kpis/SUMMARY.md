## Key KPIs (definitions / formulae)
**Inventory (the retail core):**
- **Inventory Turnover** = `COGS / average inventory` — times stock is sold & replaced per period.
- **GMROI (Gross Margin Return On Investment)** = `gross margin $ / average inventory cost` — gross
  profit per dollar of inventory investment (>1 = profitable inventory).
- **Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO)** = `365 / inventory turnover`.
- **Sell-through rate** = `units sold / units received` over a period.
**Sales / store:**
- **Same-store (comparable) sales growth** — YoY sales from stores open >=1 year (isolates organic
  growth from new-store expansion); the headline retail health metric.
- **Sales per square foot** = `net sales / selling area` — physical productivity.
- **Average Order/Transaction Value (AOV/ATV)** = `revenue / number of orders`.
**E-commerce:**
- **Conversion rate** = `transactions / sessions`.
- **Cart abandonment rate** = `1 - (completed purchases / carts created)`.
- **Customer retention / repeat purchase rate**, **CAC**, **LTV** (shared with SaaS, source 09).
