# Best parts to adopt — Fundraising & Investor Relations

## Adopt
1. **Treat fundraising as a 1-person/early-stage flagship use case** — it is where automation adds the most
   leverage for a solo founder (matching, deck, outreach, data room). Good demo surface for AutoFirm.
2. **Production automates; narrative + conversations are human.** Encode "investors spot generic output" as a
   quality gate — AI drafts, human owns substance/credibility. Avoid the AI-slop signature (mirrors §3.14).
3. **Data-room assembly + investor CRM** are concrete, automatable artifacts that tie to the document/legal
   and analytics functions.

## Reject / caveat
- Sources here are founder-marketing blogs, not peer-reviewed — lowest-rigour stream; cite as directional.
- At public-company scale, IR becomes regulated disclosure: do NOT automate disclosure autonomously.
- Never use real confidential financials/PII in AutoFirm tests of this flow (CLAUDE.md §3.12).
